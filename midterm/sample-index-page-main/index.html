Introduction to Gotham

Gotham City is located in the Northeastern United States of the DC universe and serves as the primary setting for Batman and his rogues. Gotham is famous for its gothic architecture and super high crime rates. If you happen to be getting mugged on the streets there's a chance you could get to meet Gotham's very own Batman. Gotham is the home of various colorful characters including Batman, Robin, The Joker, The Riddler, The Penguin, and many more. If you want to spend your vacation time in a city full of darkness and corruption than there's no better place to visit than Gotham

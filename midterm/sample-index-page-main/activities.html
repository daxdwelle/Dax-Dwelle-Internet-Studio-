Things to do in Gotham

Find Riddler Trophies

Throughout Gotham the villainous Riddler places trophies all over the city for people to find. In order to acquire these trophies typically you'll have to solve a puzzle or riddle. Sometimes they involve just finding the right answer to a question, but other times you may be operating a dangerous generator. These trophies aren't necessarily intended for the average citizen, but there's nothing stopping you from engaging with them. We recommend that if you're able to get one of the riddler's trophies you immediately bring them to the police, but if you want to keep one of them as a memento we won't tell.

Take a tour of Wayne Tower

For a modest price you can go on a tour of the illustrious Wayne tower the homegrounds of Gotham's own billionaire playboy Bruce Wayne. Wayne tower stands as a symbol of hope and innovation for Gotham's residents. The tour will take you through the tower's research, development, and server rooms. You can even visist the tower's observation deck that gives visitors a beautiful 360 degree view of the city. Visiting Gotham's tallest and most important building is one of the best things you can do in Gotham City.

Visit the circus

The Gotham city circus is a fun and thrilling place to spend a late night in the big city. With famous rides like the Gotham gaslight and Sub Zero there's no shortage of fun to be had. You may also compete in challenges to earn awards like plushies or the new Wayne phone. The circus also contains the largest ferris wheel in the state.

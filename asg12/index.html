Favorite Movies

These are my favorite movies because...

Almost Famous

Almost famous is the story of a young journalist writing for the rolling stone going on tour with a 70s rock band.

Spider-Man

Teenager Peter Parker gets bitten by a genetically altered spider and has to use his new powers to keep the city safe.

Zodiac

As a mysterious new killer terrorizes the citizens of San Francisco, investigators try to determine the murderer’s true identity.

Donnie Darko

A troubled teenager experiences visions of a rabbit figure telling him that the world is about to end.

Blade Runner 2049

A new blade runner uncovers a secret that has the ability to send society into chaos.

The Holdovers

A grumpy teacher forms a bond with a troubled teen as he’s forced to stay on campus during winter break to watch students with nowhere else to go.

Whiplash

An ambitious young drummer is pushed to the brink by his terrifying music teacher.

Love & Mercy

The story of how Brian Wilson created pet sounds in the 60s and recovered from his mental health crisis in the 80s.

Baby Driver

A young getaway driver wants to leave his criminal life behind after meeting the love of his life.

Scott Pilgrim vs. the World

In order to get with his new girlfriend Scott Pilgrim must defeat her 7 evil exes.

The Social Network

Learn what it took for Mark Zuckerberg to create the social network that would become facebook.

500 Days of Summer

A nonlinear romance story that explores what went wrong with the relationship between Tom and Summer.
